# **App Name**: NutriScan Expert

## Core Features:

- Secure Google Login: Allows users to securely sign in using their Google account for personalized app access.
- AI Food Recognition & Scan: Enables users to take a photo or upload an image of a food item (barcode or ingredients) for AI-driven identification.
- Personalized Nutrition Report Tool: Generates and presents a comprehensive, AI-powered nutritional analysis, including health scores (A-E, 0-100), personalized indicators, primary alerts (e.g., 'Riche en sucres!'), and a quick-look summary based on scanned food and the user's profile.
- AI-Powered Healthy Alternatives Tool: Provides context-aware suggestions for healthier alternative products based on the scanned item and user dietary needs.
- User Profile Management: Allows users to store and update personal health details such as age, gender, activity level, health goals, allergies, and dietary preferences for tailored nutritional analysis.
- Scan History Tracking: Maintains and displays a chronological list of all previously scanned products, including their name, health score, and scan date.
- Responsive App Navigation: Provides intuitive navigation with a top bar on desktop and a bottom bar on mobile for easy access to Home, Scan, and Profile sections.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A fresh, balanced green (#24995A), evoking vitality and professional reliability, chosen for contrast with a light background.
- Background color: A very subtle, almost off-white green (#ECF3F0), providing a clean and calming canvas that complements the primary color.
- Accent color: A vibrant, lively lime green (#55D400), selected to highlight calls to action and important information with strong contrast.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its modern, tech-savvy feel, suitable for titles and prominent text, reinforcing the app's scientific and expert aspect.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its clear, neutral, and highly readable quality, perfect for displaying detailed nutritional information and analysis results.
- The layout emphasizes clear visual hierarchy, with primary calls to action prominently placed on the Home page, and scan results structured for easy comprehension.
- Utilize clean, modern, line-art style icons that are easily recognizable and reinforce the concepts of health, data analysis, and food.
